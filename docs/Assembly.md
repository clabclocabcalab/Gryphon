NOP: No operation

MOV $rA (\$rB/im): Move im or $rB to $rA
MVU $rA (\$rB/im): Move im or $rB to upper half of $rA
STR $rA (\$rB/im): Store $rA to address from im or $rB
BRZ $rA (\$rB/im): Branch zero
BRN: Branch negative
SEC: Set carry
SIE: Set interrupt enabled
ADD: Add
SUB: Sub
MUL: Multiply
MHU: Multiply high unsigned
MHS: Multiply high signed
LSR: Logical shift right
LSL: Logical shift left
ASR: Arithmetic shift right
AND: And
ORR: Or
XOR: Exclusive or
NOT: Not
